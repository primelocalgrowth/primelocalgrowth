# Prime Local Growth - Newsletter Strategy & Implementation Guide

## Executive Summary

The "Local Edge" newsletter is Prime Local Growth's primary lead nurture and thought leadership channel. It converts passive website visitors into engaged prospects and turns leads into clients through consistent, valuable content.

**Goals:**
- Build email list to 1,000+ subscribers within 6 months
- Convert 2-5% of subscribers into discovery calls
- Establish authority in local business visibility
- Create evergreen content for reuse across platforms
- Support sales process with pre-qualified leads

---

## Newsletter Positioning

### Audience
Local business owners ($500K-$5M revenue) who:
- Know they need more customers
- Are currently using Google, social media, or ads without good results
- Are curious about visibility/marketing but overwhelmed
- May not be ready to hire yet but want education

### Value Proposition
"Weekly insights on Google, social media, and ads for local businesses - plus case studies and strategies to get found by customers right now."

### Voice & Tone
- **Problem-aware:** Acknowledge frustration, not judgmental
- **Educational:** Teach something valuable every time
- **Direct:** No fluff, no hype, just useful information
- **Specific:** Examples with numbers, not vague claims
- **Personal:** From Adam, direct access feeling

## Editorial Calendar & Publishing Schedule

### Publishing Frequency
**Every Thursday at 9 AM** (consistent, reliable, builds habit)

Why Thursday?
- Peak email open rates (Wed-Fri)
- Time for business owners to plan for next week
- Before weekend (more time to take action)

### Content Calendar Template

```
Week 1: Educational Tip (Pillar 1-3)
Week 2: Case Study or Client Win (Pillar 4)
Week 3: Trend/News + Your Take (Pillar 5)
Week 4: Offer or CTA (Pillar 6)
Week 5 (if applicable): Deep Dive or Advanced Strategy
```

### 4-Week Content Rotation Example

**Week 1 - Google Tip**
Subject: "The #1 Google Profile mistake 90% of businesses make"
Length: 2-3 min read
Format: Problem → Solution → Action
CTA: "Learn the framework → link to docs"

**Week 2 - Case Study**
Subject: "How [Business Type] went from 0 to $X/month (case study)"
Length: 3-4 min read
Format: Challenge → Strategy → Results → Takeaway
CTA: "Get a custom plan for your business → discovery call"

**Week 3 - Trend**
Subject: "[Platform] just changed [thing] - here's how to respond"
Length: 2-3 min read
Format: News → Why it matters → What to do
CTA: "Save this for reference → archive link"

**Week 4 - Special Offer**
Subject: "Free visibility audit (for [specific business type])"
Length: 1-2 min read
Format: Offer → How it helps → How to claim
CTA: "Schedule your audit → calendar link"

## Email Template & Design System

### Email Structure

```
PRE-HEADER TEXT (50 chars)
Should tease the main benefit or create curiosity

HEADER
- Prime Local Growth logo (left align)
- Edition number/date (right align)
- 
GREETING
"Hey [Name],"

OPENING HOOK (2-3 sentences)
- State the problem or opportunity
- Make it specific and relevant
- Create curiosity or "why should I read this?"

MAIN CONTENT (300-500 words)
- Clear, scannable structure
- 1-2 key ideas
- Practical, actionable
- Examples or numbers

CLOSE
- Sum up the key takeaway
- Single CTA (link or action)
- Personal sign-off: "— Adam"

FOOTER
- Company info
- Quick links
- Unsubscribe
```

### Design System

**Colors:**
- Primary: Gold (#FFA500)
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Dark gray (#333333)
- Links: Gold (#FFA500)
- Accent boxes: Light gold background (#FFF8DC)

**Typography:**
- Subject line: Clear benefit or curiosity (no all caps unless special)
- Main headline: Bold, problem-focused
- Body: 14-16px, readable
- CTA button: Gold background, rounded corners, white text

**Layout:**
- Single column (mobile first)
- Plenty of white space
- 80-100 character line length
- Scannable with subheadings
- Max 2-3 paragraphs before break

### CTA (Call-to-Action) Strategy

**Primary CTA:**
- For leads not yet qualified: "Learn more → [docs link or blog]"
- For warm leads: "Let's talk → [calendar link]"
- For clients: "Case study → [full case study link]"

**Button Copy:**
- "Get the strategy"
- "Schedule a 20-min call"
- "See the full story"
- "Learn the framework"
- Action-oriented, specific, benefit-driven

**CTA Placement:**
- 1 CTA per email (focuses attention)
- Placed after the main content
- Repeated in P.S. if important
- Never more than 2 CTAs

## Launch & Automation Sequence

### Lead Magnet: "Local Business Visibility Playbook"

**Description:** 5-7 page PDF with your proven framework
**Sections:**
1. The 3 Mistakes Killing Local Business Growth (and how to fix them)
2. Your Visibility Checklist (Is your business findable?)
3. The Google Visibility Framework (step-by-step)
4. The 30-Day Quick Win (one thing that moves the needle)
5. Next Steps (free consultation offer)

**How to Distribute:**
- Website signup form → immediately email the PDF
- Newsletter signup CTA → PDF + added to email list
- Lead magnet landing page → standalone offer

### Welcome Sequence (First 7 days)

**Email 1 (immediately): Lead Magnet Delivery**
- Subject: "Your Local Business Visibility Playbook (attached)"
- Introduce yourself
- Explain what's in the guide
- CTA: "Read it, let me know what you think"

**Email 2 (Day 2): Story + Connection**
- Subject: "Why I focus on local businesses"
- Personal story: Air Force → Six Sigma → helping local businesses
- Establish credibility and relatability
- CTA: "Get weekly insights → confirm subscription"

**Email 3 (Day 4): Quick Tip**
- Subject: "One thing that changed the game for my clients"
- Share a valuable tip immediately
- Show value upfront
- CTA: "Get weekly tips like this → main newsletter"

**Email 4 (Day 7): Offer Meeting**
- Subject: "Let's talk about your visibility"
- Remind of lead magnet
- Explain discovery call process (20 min, free, no pressure)
- CTA: "Schedule your call → calendar link"

### Weekly Newsletter (Ongoing)

**Frequency:** Every Thursday, 9 AM
**Cadence:** Following the 4-week rotation above
**Segmentation:** By interest (Google / Social / Ads / All)

### Engagement Sequence (Inactive Subscribers)

**After 30 days no opens:**
- Subject: "Did this email miss the mark?"
- Ask for feedback
- Re-offer value
- CTA: "Tell me what you want to read → link"

**After 60 days no opens:**
- Subject: "One last thing before we go"
- Explain you're removing inactive subscribers
- Last chance to stay engaged
- CTA: "Yes, keep me on the list → link"

**After 90 days no opens:**
- Remove from list (clean list = better deliverability)

## Email Platform Recommendation

### Option 1: **Substack** (Recommended for ease)
**Pros:**
- Easiest setup (5 min)
- Built-in monetization (future option)
- Great reader experience
- Analytics included

**Cons:**
- Less automation
- Limited segmentation

**Cost:** Free (or 10% revenue cut if monetized)

### Option 2: **ConvertKit** (Recommended for creators)
**Pros:**
- Great email design
- Automation/sequences included
- Subscriber segmentation
- Growth tools

**Cons:**
- $29+/month minimum
- Steeper learning curve

**Cost:** $29-$79/month depending on subscribers

### Option 3: **Brevo** (Recommended for businesses)
**Pros:**
- Free up to 300 emails/day
- Advanced automation
- CRM features included
- Integrates with Wave (your invoicing tool!)

**Cons:**
- UI is less intuitive
- Spam folder risk if not set up right

**Cost:** Free tier, then $20+/month

**RECOMMENDATION: Start with Substack (easiest), migrate to Brevo when you have 500+ subscribers (integrates with Wave)**

## Metrics & Success Tracking

### Key Metrics to Track

**Subscriber Metrics:**
- New subscribers per week
- Subscriber growth rate
- List size
- Unsubscribe rate (should be <0.5% per email)

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Open rate (target: 35-45%)
- Click rate (target: 3-5%)
- Reply rate (target: 1-2%)
- Forward/share rate

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Subscribers who become leads
- Lead-to-discovery-call conversion
- Discovery call to proposal rate
- Newsletter subscribers in closed clients (should be 80%+)

**Content Metrics:**
- Topic performance (which topics get most opens/clicks)
- Subject line performance
- CTA performance
- Time of send optimization

### Success Targets

**Month 1:** 50 subscribers (from website + launch promotion)
**Month 2:** 125 subscribers (3-4 per week new)
**Month 3:** 200 subscribers (5-7 per week new)
**Month 6:** 500 subscribers
**Year 1:** 1,000+ subscribers

**Conversion Target:** 2-5% of subscribers = discovery calls
- 500 subscribers × 3% = 15 calls/month
- 15 calls × 30% close rate = 4-5 new clients/month

## FAQ

**Q: How often should we send?**
A: Once per week, same day/time. Consistency builds habits.

**Q: What if engagement drops?**
A: Check 1) send time, 2) subject line quality, 3) content relevance, 4) email design/rendering

**Q: How do we grow the list?**
A: Website signups (forms, footer, exit intent), social media promotion, lead magnet, partnerships

**Q: Should we segment?**
A: Yes, by interest (Google / Social / Ads) once you have 200+ subscribers

**Q: What's the unsubscribe rate?**
A: 0.5% per email is normal. >1% means content isn't resonating.

**Q: How do we measure ROI?**
A: Track newsletter subscribers → discovery calls → closed clients. Attribution is key.
