FAQ

Questions local business owners ask first.

How long does local search improvement take?

Initial visibility fixes can happen quickly, but competitive ranking movement usually builds over 30 to 90 days.

Can I rank without ads?

Yes. Ads are optional. Organic local visibility depends on profile quality, reviews, website trust, location signals, and authority.

Why am I not showing up on Google?

Common reasons include a weak Google profile, inconsistent business information, not enough reviews, and competitors with stronger local signals.

Do I need a website?

A strong Google profile can drive calls, but a trustworthy website helps customers choose you once they click.

How do reviews affect rankings?

Reviews help customers trust you and give Google signals about activity, relevance, and reputation.

What makes Prime Local Growth different?

We focus on local Google visibility systems, not broad marketing packages or confusing SEO jargon.
