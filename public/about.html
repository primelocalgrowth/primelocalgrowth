Background

20+ years in the U.S. Air Force. Now solving a different kind of logistics problem.

In the Air Force, execution meant clarity of mission, no wasted steps, and accountability for outcomes. Local SEO for service businesses works the same way — there is a gap between where your business shows up on Google and where it should be, and there is a specific sequence of steps to close it.

I started Prime Local Growth because I saw small business owners getting sold complexity when what they needed was execution. Agencies with account managers and deliverables decks. No one actually fixing the profile, building the reviews, or making the phone ring more.

I do not subcontract. I do not outsource. Every audit, every Google post, every review response, every monthly report — that is me. Based in Cibolo, TX, serving service businesses across the San Antonio metro and beyond.
