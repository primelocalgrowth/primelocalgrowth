The business case

Your online presence is part of your business value.

Buyers and successors look at Google reviews, ranking position, and listing activity before they make decisions. A strong, well-maintained profile tells them the business is legitimate, trusted, and thriving. It smooths the transition, supports a higher sale price, and gives the new owner a foundation to build from. A neglected listing - outdated hours, unanswered reviews, months of silence - does the opposite. It raises doubts, invites lowball offers, and can spook a serious buyer entirely.

“The listing you built took years. It takes 90 days of neglect to undo it.”
