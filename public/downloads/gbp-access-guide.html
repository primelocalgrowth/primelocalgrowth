01
Go to Your Google Business Profile
Takes about 60 seconds
  • 1
    Open your web browser and go to:
    business.google.com
  • 2
    Sign in using the Google account that owns your business listing. This is usually the Gmail you used when you first set up the profile.
  • 3
    You will see your business dashboard. Your business name should appear at the top. You are in the right place.
💡
Pro Tip -- Use the right Google account
Check the profile icon in the top right corner. Make sure it shows the email address associated with your business -- not a personal Gmail. If you're not sure which account owns the profile, try both and see which one shows the business dashboard.
⚠️
Don't see your business?
You may be signed into the wrong Google account. Click the profile icon in the top right, switch accounts, and try again. If the profile was never created or claimed, contact us first -- we'll handle that step together.
02
Open Business Profile Settings
Takes about 30 seconds
  • 1
    From your dashboard at business.google.com, locate your business listing.
  • 2
    Click the three-dot menu (⋮) next to your business name, then select "Business Profile settings."
  • 3
    In the settings panel that opens, click "Managers." This is the access control center for your profile.
💡
Alternative path -- works on desktop too
Search your exact business name on Google while signed in. Click "Edit profile" in the result panel. Then look for "Managers" in the left-side menu. Both paths lead to the same place.
03
Add a New User
Takes about 60 seconds
  • 1
    On the Managers page, click the person+ icon (Add user) in the top right corner of the panel.
  • 2
    A dialog box will appear asking for an email address. Enter exactly the address below -- copy and paste to avoid typos:
Enter this email address exactly
primelocalgrowth@gmail.com
Copy and paste to avoid typos
  • 3
    After entering the email, you will be asked to select a role for this user. Do not click Invite yet -- see Step 4 first.
⚠️
Type the email carefully
If the email address is entered incorrectly, the invite will go to the wrong person or fail entirely. Copy and paste directly from this document to be safe.
04
Select "Manager" Permission
Critical -- choose the correct role
  • 1
    In the role dropdown, you will see three options: Owner, Manager, and Site Manager.
  • 2
    Select "Manager." This gives us everything we need to manage your profile -- without any ability to delete it or remove you.
  • 3
    Click "Invite." Google will send an invitation to our email. You will see a confirmation message on screen.
✅
Manager vs. Site Manager -- what's the difference?
"Site Manager" is a limited role that can only manage posts. "Manager" is what we need -- it includes photo uploads, review responses, analytics access, and profile edits. Always select Manager, not Site Manager.
🚫
Do not select "Owner"
The Owner role gives full administrative control, including the ability to delete the profile and remove you. We only need Manager. If you accidentally select Owner, cancel the invite and start Step 3 again.
05
We Accept and Confirm
Nothing more needed from you
  • 1
    After you click Invite, Google sends a notification to primelocalgrowth@gmail.com. The invitation expires after 30 days if not accepted.
  • 2
    We will accept the invitation within 24 hours and send you a confirmation that access is connected and we've started working.
  • 3
    You are done. No further action needed. We will handle all profile activity from here and keep you updated on what we do.
📬
Didn't hear from us within 24 hours?
Text or call us at 210-646-1436, or email primelocalgrowth@gmail.com. We'll confirm receipt and check that the invite came through correctly.

How to verify access was granted
Go back to Business Profile settings → Managers. You should see "primelocalgrowth@gmail.com" listed with the role "Manager." If it shows "Pending," we haven't accepted yet -- that's normal. Check back in 24 hours.
