Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Prime Local Growth ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website primelocalgrowth.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site or submit your contact information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Business type or industry

We also collect basic analytics data such as page views and traffic sources through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Send you the Local Visibility Jumpstart Kit you requested
  • Follow up with educational emails about local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization
  • Respond to your questions or requests personally
  • Send occasional marketing emails related to our services

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Consent to Email Communications

By submitting your contact information through any form on this website, you consent to receive email communications from Prime Local Growth, including your requested audit, follow-up educational content about local SEO, and occasional marketing messages about our services. You may withdraw this consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly. We use Beehiiv to manage our email list.

Data Storage

Your contact information is stored securely in Google Sheets and our email platform (Beehiiv). We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Beehiiv, email newsletter platform (privacy policy)
  • Resend, transactional email delivery (privacy policy)
  • Stripe, payment processing. We do not store your payment card data; it is handled directly by Stripe (privacy policy)
  • Google Sheets, lead data storage (privacy policy)
  • Vercel, website hosting and analytics (privacy policy)
  • Google Analytics (GA4), website usage analytics, loaded only with your consent (privacy policy)

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 is only activated after you give explicit consent via the cookie banner shown on your first visit. You may accept or decline analytics cookies at any time. We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies of any kind.

If you decline analytics cookies, your visit will not be tracked by GA4. Basic server-side analytics (page load counts, not linked to personal identity) may still be collected by our hosting provider, Vercel.

You can also disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. See allaboutcookies.org for guidance.

Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to email marketing at any time (unsubscribe link in every email)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by declining cookies or disabling them in your browser

To exercise any of these rights, email us at adam@primelocalgrowth.com. We will respond within 30 days.

CAN-SPAM Compliance

All marketing emails we send comply with the CAN-SPAM Act. Every email includes a clear identification of Prime Local Growth as the sender, a valid physical address (Cibolo, TX 78108), and a one-click unsubscribe mechanism. We honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days.

Children's Privacy

Our services are directed at business owners and are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, contact us immediately at adam@primelocalgrowth.com and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy
