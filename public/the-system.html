The real problem

Your competitor is not better. He just shows up first.

When a customer searches "plumber near me" or "HVAC Cibolo," Google shows the businesses it trusts most — not the ones that do the best work. If your profile is incomplete, your reviews are thin, or your information is inconsistent, Google pushes you down. The customer calls whoever is at the top. Your phone is quiet. Theirs is not.

That is a fixable problem. Here is exactly how we fix it.

The Process

Step by step — what actually happens.

1
Phase 1 · Week 1–4

We find what is broken and fix it.

Adam audits your Google profile, your reviews, your website, and your top three local competitors. You get a plain-English report showing exactly what is costing you calls — then we fix it.

  • Google profile rebuilt from scratch if needed
  • Business info checked for accuracy across the web
  • Review link set up so customers can actually leave one
  • Website checked for trust and call-conversion issues
  • Competitor gap snapshot — where they are beating you and why
Your ask: Approve the design preview in Week 1. Adam handles everything else.
2
Phase 2 · Every Month

We keep you visible so competitors cannot take your spot back.

Visibility decays. Google rewards businesses that stay active — fresh posts, new reviews, updated information. We handle all of it so you do not have to think about it.

  • Weekly Google posts and photo updates
  • Every new review gets a real response, same day
  • SMS review push to your existing customers (Adam-run)
  • Competitor alerts — if someone moves on you, we know first
  • One plain-English summary every month: what changed, what is next
Your ask: Respond to urgent review flags within 24 hours. Otherwise hands-off.
3
Growth Add-Ons · As Needed

We expand your reach when you are ready to grow.

Once your core visibility is solid, there is room to push further — more cities, faster review growth, deeper competitive intel. These are optional, one-time additions, not a subscription upgrade.

  • Service area expansion to 3 new target cities
  • 30-day review acceleration campaign via SMS
  • Competitor audit — their weaknesses, your opportunity
Only worth doing after Phase 1 has been running for 30 days.
