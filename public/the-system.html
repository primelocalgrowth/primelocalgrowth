The real problem

Your competitor is not better. They just show up first.

Google does not know who does the best work. It shows whoever it trusts most. If your profile is incomplete, your reviews are thin, or your information is inconsistent, Google pushes you down. The customer calls whoever is at the top. Your phone is quiet. Theirs is not.

That is a fixable problem. Here is exactly how we fix it.

Step by step — what actually happens.

1
Phase 1 · Week 1–4

We find what is broken and fix it.

Adam audits your Google profile, your reviews, your website, and your top three local competitors. You get a plain-English report showing exactly what is costing you calls — then we fix it.

  • Google profile rebuilt from scratch if needed
  • Business info checked for accuracy across the web
  • Review link set up so customers can actually leave one

  • Competitor gap snapshot — where they are beating you and why
